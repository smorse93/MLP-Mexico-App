Student Learning Platform

 Welcome students! We have developed a video series for you  to follow along with that goes through the various modules of our program. Follow along module by module in order to complete your MLP education.  There are a total of 25 modules, all of which are in Spanish. English materials can be found here. You can take your business dreams to the next step and use the DreamBuilder tool to help you along your journey in creating a business.

Appendices

Module 11

How many times have you said that you could not start your business because you have no money? In this activity we take a new look at the most important aspects of a business.

Module 12

Sometimes we believe our clients do not value what we give them. This activity is a moment of reflection to find out what your customers value and why it is important to know. You will also learn that this undertaking is a constant give and take between you and many others.

Module 13

Many women have not started their businesses because they are afraid they will not sell well, that they won't recover their investment, or have a fear of borrowing. What are you afraid of? What has prevented you from undertaking a business?

Module 14

The key is to be informed, prepared and organized. Know many tips on what you must do to lose the fear to undertake a business. 

Module 15

Autonomy. It is the magic word we need to undertake. If we do not have it, it is unlikely that our venture will last long. 

​Module 16

If you could pick from 3 deals, what would you take? If you had to choose between those 3, what should you consider to choose the best idea? Here are some tips.

Module 17

Here we will show you how to do market research. You will learn the general aspects most critical to this tool.

Module 18

We will give you a basic guide on how to determine the cost of making the product or service you offer. Based on that cost, the value, and profile of your consumers.

Module 19

We suggest basic accounting actions that you must carry out in your business.

Module 20

Learn the main aspects that you must communicate in your marketing material.
